About Us

Professional teleprompting for productions that can't afford a second take.

Our Story

Pennsylvania Teleprompter provides professional teleprompting services for productions of every scale—from national conferences and broadcast sets to live streams and executive keynotes. Our operators are seasoned specialists who understand timing, pace, and presence.

We handle every part of the process—setup, script integration, and live operation—so your team stays focused on message and delivery. With current-generation gear and proven show control methods, we ensure smooth, confident performances in any environment.

Our Approach

Scripts flow, speakers stay composed, and the message lands with confidence and impact. That's not a promise—it's what we deliver on every show.

We work with production teams, agencies, and in-house creators who need flawless execution on deadline. Current-generation equipment, proven show control methods, and extensive experience across thousands of shows.
