## Why Virtual Events Use Teleprompters

✅ **On-camera confidence** — Presenting to camera is different than to audience
✅ **Technical complexity** — Virtual events have many moving parts to manage
✅ **Professional appearance** — Home/office setup must look polished
✅ **Multiple speakers** — Many virtual events have speaker rotations
✅ **Production integration** — Teleprompter integrates with streaming software
