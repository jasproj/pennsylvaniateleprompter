## Miami Teleprompter FAQ

**Q: Do I need to rehearse with the operator?**
A: Yes, even 30 minutes is valuable. Rehearsal helps the operator understand your pace and delivery style.

**Q: What if I'm not comfortable with teleprompters?**
A: Our operators are experienced coaches. We'll help you find a comfortable delivery style that looks natural.

**Q: Can you handle bilingual/multilingual events?**
A: Yes. We work with interpreters and can format scripts for multiple languages.

**Q: What if the script needs to change during the event?**
A: Notify us ASAP. Professional operators can usually accommodate changes quickly.

**Q: Will the audience see the teleprompter?**
A: Depends on the system. Glass teleprompters are invisible. Monitor systems are visible but standard in professional events. We'll recommend what's best.

**Q: What if we're recording the event?**
A: We coordinate with your video team to ensure teleprompter systems don't interfere with camera work.
