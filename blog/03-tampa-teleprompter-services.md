A: Depends on the system. Glass teleprompters are invisible. Monitor systems are visible but professional. We'll recommend what works best for your venue.

**Q: What if our venue is outdoors?**
A: We handle outdoor Tampa events regularly. Professional systems are weatherproofed and brightness-optimized for outdoor use.

**Q: Do we need to rehearse with the operator?**
A: Strongly recommended. Even 30 minutes of rehearsal helps the operator learn your speaker's pace and delivery style.
