## Real Estate Teleprompter Best Practices

**Before Event:**
- Gather all property specs, financial details, legal messaging
- Write script with exact numbers and compliance-approved language
- Rehearse with speaker to ensure confidence
- Verify all technical details (venue, lighting, sound)

**During Event:**
- Operator adjusts for speaker pace
- Real-time display of updated information
- Professional appearance maintained
- Multiple speakers managed seamlessly

**Result:** Professional, credible, compelling presentation
